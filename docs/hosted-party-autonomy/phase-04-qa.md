# Phase 4 QA: Support Roles

## Goal

Verify party support is tactically useful and does not create regressions in
ambient bot grouping.

## Checks

- Verify heal-capable classes prioritize wounded or threatened allies.
- Verify tank-capable classes protect fragile allies.
- Verify buff-capable classes use practical pre-pull buffs.
- Verify focus fire works while movement is paused for follow or regroup.
- Verify ambient-bot-led and real-player-led parties remain distinct.
